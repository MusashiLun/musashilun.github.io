Meme and Reflection

Evolution involves the accumulation of morphological changes, and pets have undergone artificial selection. If two species look alike as a result of artificial selection, can this be considered a form of convergent evolution to some extent?

I guess this depends on how you define evolution! If your definition includes only natural selection, then I guess it would be excluded, but if you included artificial selection in that, then it would be included. I think most evolutionary biologists use definitions that rely on evolution by natural selection though :)
